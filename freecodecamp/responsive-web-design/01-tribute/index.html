About Master Elon

Timeline

  • 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1981: Taught himself computer programming
  • 1983: At age 12, created Blaster, a BASIC video game, and sold it for 500$.
  • 1989: Moved to Canada to live with his mother.
  • 1992: Studied business and physics in University of Pennsylvania, got an undergraduate in economics and a bachelor's degree in physics.
  • 1995: Went to Stanford to pursue a PhD, but left after two days.
  • 1995: Founded Zip2, software company
  • 1999: Sold shares in Zip2 for $22M, then founded X.com, a financial services company.
  • 2002: Founded Space Exploration Technologies, or SpaceX.
  • 2006: Co-founded SolarCity, providing solar power systems across the US.
  • 2008: Assumed leadership of Tesla Motors.
  • 2015: Created OpenAI, to develop artificial intelligence in a way that is safe and beneficial to humanity.
  • 2016: Co-founded Neuralink, to create devices that can interface with the human brain.
